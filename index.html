About Me:

I am a thorough and detail oriented problem solver, transitioning from interior design to development. With over 8 years of experience in a large corporate environment, I had worked on a variety of requests, ranging from delivering a chair from inventory to coordinating the installation of new furniture for an entire floor. Even though I was working in design, something was missing.

I have always had the urge to create and recently became interested in computer science after an introductory weekend course. With the encouragement of my husband, who is a software engineer, and the support of family and friends, I realized that it was time for a fresh start and began my new career path.

As a developer with a design background, my goal is to create applications that are both streamlined and beautiful, providing an elegant solution for the client.
